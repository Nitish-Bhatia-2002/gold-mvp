Gold Rate in India Today — Explained

Indian gold prices start from the global gold price (XAUUSD), then get converted to INR, and adjusted for purity, making charges, and GST.

1. Global Price (Spot)

The starting point is the international spot price of gold, quoted in USD per troy ounce. Your main page uses this via our /api/gold endpoint.

2. Convert USD to INR

If USD/INR rises, Indian gold prices rise even if global gold is flat.

3. Purity (24k vs 22k vs 18k)

24k = pure (999), 22k = 916 (used for ornaments), 18k for designer jewellery.

4. Making Charges

Jewellers add 5 – 15 % for labour/design work.

5. GST

Usually 3 % on gold + 5 % on making charges.
