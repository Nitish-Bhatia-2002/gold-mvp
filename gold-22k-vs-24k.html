22K vs 24K Gold in India

24K is pure but soft; 22K is stronger and used for most jewellery. Here’s how they differ.

What is 24K?

24K = 99.9 % pure gold. Best for bars and coins.

What is 22K?

22K = 91.6 % gold (hence 916). Mixed with other metals to make it harder for daily wear.

Price Difference

22K ≈ 24K × 0.916 (then add making + GST). Your calculator does this exactly.

Which to buy?

  • Investment → 24K bar/coin
  • Jewellery → 22K is practical
  • Designer pieces → 18K sometimes used
